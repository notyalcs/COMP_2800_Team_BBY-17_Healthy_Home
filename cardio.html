The Importance of Cardio

Increased Heart Health

Your heart is a muscle, and without proper conditioning it can become weak. 150 minutes of cardio per week is recommended to reduce heart related health risks.

Improved Metabolism

Cardiovascular stimulation increases the bodies metabolism, which aids in weight loss and weight maintenance. High intensity interval training is especially efficient at increasing metabolic rates.

Improved Sleep Quality and Duration

Regular cardiovascular exercise has been found to increase the quality and duration of sleep in individuals with chronic sleep problems.

Additional Benfits

Additional benefits associated with cardiovascular activity include improved lung capacity, decreased stress, improved alertness and mood, strengthened immune system, among many more wuality of life benfits.
