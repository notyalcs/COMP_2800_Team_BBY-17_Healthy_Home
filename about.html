The Ergomaniacs

Created by the lords of chaos, we are the best COMP 2800 group from the British Columbia Insitute of Technology, the Ergomaniacs. We are passionate about supporting people that are stuck at home during these unprecedented times. As a group, we push ourselves to produce the highest level products that we can while supporting one another. Our flagship product is a web application called Healthy Home. We provide users a platform to encourage activities and healthy living directed towards people that are in a stagnant lifestyle. We hope that everyone is staying safe and is keeping a Healthy Home!
